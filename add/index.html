Add Bastion to Discord

Add Bastion to Discord, and give awesome perks to your Discord server.

Public Bastion

Add the public Bastion to your Discord servers. Music is disabled in the public Bastion due to hosting & maintenace cost for so many Discord servers.

Private Bastion

Host an instance of Bastion yourself on your system. You get to experience all the features of Bastion; play high quality music, configure it the way you want, custom avatar, username are just the tip of the iceberg. Whole set of bot owner only commands at the press of your keyboard! You're responsible for updating your instance whenever new releases come out and also for keeping it online 24x7.

Hosted Bastion

Private Bastion, hosted by us, for you! Everything you get with a private instance of Bastion, without the hassel of keeping it online and updated. We'll do that for you. This is only available as a reward to a few Patreon Tiers, check Patron for more details.
